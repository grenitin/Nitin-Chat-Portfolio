HealthCare: Simplifying Doctor Discovery & Appointments.

A centralized healthcare platform designed to bridge the gap between patients and trusted medical professionals through transparent, data-driven discovery.

Duration 4 Weeks
Role Lead UX Designer
0

Project Overview

Solving the fragmented discovery crisis for patients and medical professionals through a unified healthcare ecosystem.

HealthCare addresses the fragmented discovery challenges faced by patients in India. While urgent care is available, finding trusted specialists and managing records remains a stressful manual process.

The goal was to evolve healthcare from a fragmented experience into a unified, transparent ecosystem, integrating verified doctor profiles and real-time appointment tracking into a single, reliable platform.

The Problem

Decision Fatigue

Users struggle with 'which doctor to choose' every single time, leading to anxiety and expensive last-minute choices.

Fragmented Supply

Medical professionals lack digital visibility and scalable tools to manage patient queues efficiently.

E

Let's Empathize

Understanding the human behavior, market constraints, and foundational insights that drive the problem space.

Business Understanding

Business Goal

Increase recurring revenue by converting patients into long-term health subscribers.

Constraints

Must leverage verified medical databases while creating a seamless flow for real-time appointment slot discovery and management.

Market Opportunity

Tapping into the $15B unorganized Indian medical services market with a scalable, digital trust layer.

Competitive Analysis

Aggregators (Practo/1mg)

Pros: Large doctor database, digital booking.

Cons: Fragmented UI, overwhelming results, trust issues with sponsored listings.

Hospital Portals (Apollo/Fortis)

Pros: High trust, ecosystem of services.

Cons: Limited to specific networks, rigid scheduling, manual report handling.

Word-of-Mouth

Pros: High personal trust, verified experience.

Cons: Extremely slow discovery, subjective, not scalable for emergencies.

Key Insights

Trust Deficit

Users are skeptical of generic ratings and want verified patient stories and transparent fee breakdowns before booking.

Appointment Anxiety

68% of users expressed frustration over unpredictable wait times even after securing a confirmed digital slot.

Record Fragmentation

Patients struggle to manage history across different specialists, leading to redundant tests and loss of critical medical data.
