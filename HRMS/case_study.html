Project Overview

Unifying fragmented HR workflows into a scalable, in-house system for mid-sized product companies.

Small to mid-sized product-based companies are struggling with rigid third-party HRMS solutions that fail to align with evolving workflows. These systems introduce high dependency costs and poor adaptability across core functions.

Careline was designed to solve this by providing a modular, intuitive, and scalable in-house experience that supports Attendance, Tickets, Team Management, and Leaves—all within a unified ecosystem.

The Problem

Fragmented Ecosystem

Scattered information architecture across modules leads to high cognitive load and frequent errors.

Operational Inefficiency

Lack of intuitive navigation and inconsistent patterns delay task completion for HRs and managers.
